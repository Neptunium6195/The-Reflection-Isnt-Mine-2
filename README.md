# visualNovel2

The Reflection Isn't Mine is a visual novel with interactive mini games that tells the story of a recently recruited young maid named Ellia, who seeks to solve the mystery of her lost lover, Charles. However, she begins to find strange reflections, symbols, and illusions in the mirror in the estate, and she desperately tries to escape them, possibly saving her lover on the way.

It was made on godot using the dialogic plugin and you can play it here: https://neptune6195.itch.io/the-reflection-isnt-mine-2
